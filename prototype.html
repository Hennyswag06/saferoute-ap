Application Prototype

Designed to provide fast, reliable emergency assistance when it matters most.

The Problem

Many people find themselves in emergencies where they need immediate help but cannot quickly contact emergency services or share their location. Delays can put lives at risk.



The Solution

SafeRoute provides users with one-touch emergency alerts, real-time GPS tracking, safe route navigation, and instant communication with trusted contacts.

🚨 SOS Button

Instantly sends alerts to emergency contacts with your location.

📍 Live GPS

Shares your real-time location during emergencies.

🛡 Safe Routes

Provides safer navigation options based on location and conditions.

🏥 Emergency Services

Quick access to nearby hospitals, police stations, and shelters.

Target Users

Students, travelers, families, elderly individuals, military veterans, and anyone who wants added personal safety and peace of mind.
