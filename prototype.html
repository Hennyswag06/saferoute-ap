Problem Statement

During emergencies, many individuals experience delays in contacting help and sharing their location.

Solution

SafeRoute provides immediate emergency alerts, location sharing, and navigation to safe locations.

Main Features

  • SOS Emergency Button
  • Live GPS Tracking
  • Trusted Contact Alerts
  • Safe Route Recommendations
  • Emergency Resource Locator

Target Users

  • Students
  • Travelers
  • Families
  • Elderly Individuals
  • Night-Shift Workers
