The creator behind the SafeRoute Emergency Assistance App.

About Me

My name is Jada Hammond, and I created SafeRoute as part of my Information Technology studies. The purpose of this application is to improve personal safety by helping users quickly access emergency assistance, share their location, and navigate to safe destinations during emergencies.



Why I Created SafeRoute

In today's world, personal safety is more important than ever. Many people face situations where they need immediate assistance but may not have time to search for help. SafeRoute provides a simple solution that connects users with emergency contacts and resources when every second counts.
