User Guide

Learn how to use SafeRoute to stay safe and connected.

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to begin using SafeRoute:

Step 1

Create your SafeRoute account using your email address and password.

Step 2

Add trusted emergency contacts such as family members and friends.

Step 3

Enable GPS location services for emergency tracking.

Step 4

Customize your emergency settings and notifications.

Using the SOS Feature

When an emergency occurs, simply press the SOS button. SafeRoute will immediately send your location and emergency alert to your selected contacts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does SafeRoute track my location all the time?

A: No. Location sharing is only activated when enabled or during an emergency.


Q: Can I add multiple emergency contacts?

A: Yes. Users can add several trusted contacts.


Q: Can I use SafeRoute while traveling?

A: Absolutely. SafeRoute is designed to work wherever emergency assistance may be needed.
