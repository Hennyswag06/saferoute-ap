Welcome to SafeRoute

SafeRoute is an emergency assistance application designed to help users stay safe through instant SOS alerts, live location sharing, and access to nearby emergency services.

Key Features

  • One-Touch SOS Alerts
  • Real-Time Location Sharing
  • Safe Route Navigation
  • Emergency Contact Notifications
  • Nearby Hospitals and Police Stations
